Ghana

Ghana is a country situated in West Africa, located on the Gulf of Guinea and the Atlantic Ocean. It shares borders with Ivory Coast to the west, Burkina Faso to the north, and Togo to the east. The capital city of Ghana is Accra, which is a coastal city. Ghana is a constitutional democracy with a population of 28,102,471 and covers an area of 92,100 square miles. The country's official languages are English, Akan, Moshi-Dagomba, Ewe, and Ga, and its currency is the Cedi
